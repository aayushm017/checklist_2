Check List for UC005-Modify Reference Tables Structure

Level-0: Agency Steward

  • Raise request for creating a new Lookup table in RDM.
  • Provide the valid table name and table structure details.
  • Check the DQ validation rules while providing the table.
  • Once complete providing input values, then click on Submit button.

Level-1: RDM Governance Lead

  • Verify the define request form.
  • Task Assign to RDM Governance team.
  • RDM Governance Lead Review and Approve the request. Once Approved, the task goes to the RDM Governance Team (Level 2).
  • RDM Governance Lead Review and Reject the request. Once rejected, the task goes to the Agency Steward (Level 0).

Level-2: RDM Governance Team

  • RDM Governance Team implements the new table as per Agency Data Steward request form details.
  • Open the RDM tool and create the new table in Dev.
  • Open the provisioning tool and create the new Business Entity and Business Entity View.
  • Open the transformation Business Entity TO Business Entity VIEW and Business Entity VIEW TO Business Entity.
  • UI Layout and workflow design configuration in Layout Designer.
  • Once all changes are completed, click on Apply and Publish button.
  • Login E360 application, open task manager and click Approve button; the task goes to the Closed state.
  • Once rejected, the task goes to the RDM Governance Lead (Level 1).
